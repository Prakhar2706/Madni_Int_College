Barnali Chetia

Assistant Professor(Linguistics)

Educational Qualifications

  • Ph.D (Jawaharlal Nehru University, 2014)
  • M.Phil (Jawaharlal Nehru University, 2010)
  • M.A (Jawaharlal Nehru University, 2007)
  • UGC-NET (2007)
  • PGDM-Print Journalism (IMSIT, New Delhi, 2005)

Research interests

Applied Linguistics, Sociolinguistics, Second Language Acquisition, Discourse Analysis, Gender Studies, Technical Communication.

Member of Professional bodies

Life Member- Linguistic Society of India
